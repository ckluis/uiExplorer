Purpose

Explore non-intrusive inline indicators for surfacing cross-page configuration issues in enterprise software. Issues are discovered naturally while working, with contextual popovers providing progressive disclosure of details and remediation paths.

Variant Focus

"Contextual Whisper" — Subtle warning dots appear next to affected form fields and sections. Users discover issues in the natural flow of their work rather than being interrupted by modals or panels. Hovering reveals popovers with context, relationship paths, and links to related settings. The approach minimizes disruption while ensuring issues are visible to those who need them.

v6: "Contextual Whisper" — Inline Indicators with Popovers

Tagline: "This field is affected by settings elsewhere."

Core Concept: Non-intrusive inline indicators that appear next to affected form fields and sections. Users discover issues naturally while working, rather than being interrupted. Hovering/clicking reveals popovers with context and links to related settings.

Exposure Method: Subtle inline warning dots (small amber circles) next to form fields/sections. No central list — issues surface in context.

Presentation Style: Tooltips/popovers that expand on hover or click. Progressive disclosure of details.

Target Persona: Regular user mid-task. Doesn't want interruption but appreciates contextual help. Discovers issues naturally.

Platform: Web (desktop), simplified mobile.

Key UI Elements: Small warning dots (8px amber circles with pulse animation). Popover on hover/focus (appears above/below field). Progressive disclosure ("Show more" expands). "View related setting" deep links. Dismiss/snooze options in popover. Severity colors (amber for warning, red for critical). Connection lines showing relationship (CSS dashed borders).

Sample Form Context (Settings page with fields): "Company Name" field — normal. "Default Currency" field — [warning dot] "This conflicts with 3 customer records in USD". "Tax Rate" field — [warning dot] "Inherited from: Organization Settings, last changed 2 days ago". "Integration Endpoint" field — [critical dot] "Webhook delivery failing since yesterday".

Color/Mood: Subtle, unobtrusive. Amber/orange for warnings that don't scream. Clean form aesthetic with whispered hints.

Interaction Patterns: :hover on dots shows popover. :focus-within keeps popover visible. Checkbox for "Show more" expansion. Smooth max-height transitions for reveal.

Device Preview: Include desktop/mobile/fullscreen switcher.
