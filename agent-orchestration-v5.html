Human/Agent Orchestration

v5 Fully Autonomous

Purpose

Exploring agent orchestration as ocean depth zones — the human at the sunlit surface, orchestrator in the twilight zone, sub-agents in the midnight zone, deep specialists in the abyss. Part of a 10-variant research series.

Variant Focus

Tidal Pool — vertical cross-section of ocean. Agents are sea creatures appropriate to their depth. Resources flow down as “marine snow” (task decomposition) and results rise as “thermal vents” (completed work). Bioluminescent signal chains carry questions upward.
