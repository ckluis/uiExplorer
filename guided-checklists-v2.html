v2: "Gentle Guide" — Educational Wizard Modal

Tagline: "Let me show you why this matters."

Core Concept: An educational wizard-style modal for new users in their first 30 days. Each issue is presented as a learning opportunity with context about WHY it matters, not just how to fix it. Celebratory states when issues are resolved.

Exposure Method: Pulsing beacon widgets appear on the page near problematic areas. A "Setup Assistant" modal that walks through issues one at a time with a progress stepper.

Presentation Style: Centered modal (max 520px) with progress stepper at top. Each step shows one issue with illustration, explanation, and action. Confetti/celebration on completion.

Target Persona: New user, first 30 days. Needs hand-holding, educational context. Doesn't know the system yet.

Platform: Web, with mobile-friendly modal sizing.

Key UI Elements: Progress stepper (Step 2 of 5), friendly illustration panels (CSS gradients representing concepts), "Why This Matters" expandable section, clear action button ("Fix This" / "Learn More" / "Skip for Now"), encouraging tone throughout, completion celebration with confetti animation (CSS keyframes), "You're doing great!" motivational messages, beacon pulse animation on trigger buttons.

Sample Checklist Items: 1. "Complete Your Profile" — Your team needs to know who you are. 2. "Connect Your Calendar" — So we can help you schedule better. 3. "Set Your Notification Preferences" — Control how we reach you. 4. "Invite Your First Team Member" — Collaboration starts here. 5. "Review Security Settings" — Keep your account safe.

Color/Mood: Warm, friendly, encouraging. Soft purples and greens. Rounded corners. Approachable typography.

Interaction Patterns: Radio buttons for step navigation, checkbox for "Why This Matters" expand, CSS confetti animation on last step, pulse animation on beacons.

Device Preview: Include desktop/mobile/fullscreen switcher.
