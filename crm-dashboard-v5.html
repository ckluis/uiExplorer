Purpose

Explore an ambient/invisible CRM paradigm where CRM intelligence surfaces contextually within a familiar Gmail-like email inbox, rather than existing as a separate destination. The CRM you never open.

Variant Focus

"Context Layer" — Email-first CRM overlay. CRM data appears as inline enrichment badges, contact intelligence sidebars, smart priority highlighting, and ghost notifications. The inbox looks entirely familiar; the CRM is the invisible layer.

Prompt

This version was generated autonomously by a sub-agent. The prompt below is the exact prompt given to the build agent — it was crafted by the orchestrating agent based on the origination prompt and research phase.

v5: "Context Layer" — Ambient/Invisible CRM

Tagline: "The CRM you never open."
Core metaphor: A heads-up display — CRM intelligence surfaces on top of email rather than being a separate destination.

Layout: Simulates a standard email inbox (Gmail-like) taking up the full window. CRM data appears as contextual overlays: right sidebar panel (~280px) showing contact intelligence when an email is selected, inline badges on email rows (deal stage, lead score), and subtle highlights on emails needing attention.

Key UI elements: Email row enrichment badges (deal stage, temperature). Contact intelligence sidebar on selection. Smart priority highlighting (warm left-border). Inline action buttons. Ghost notifications ("You haven't contacted Sarah Chen in 12 days"). Email compose enrichment. Meeting detection badges. Standard email UI chrome.

Color/mood: Standard neutral email interface. CRM overlays use soft indigo/teal accent. Ghost notifications in desaturated warm tones. Invisible until needed, then quietly helpful.

Role toggle: Solo Agent sees personal history and reminders. Team Lead gains "Team Activity" tab and team alerts. Brokerage Admin gets audit view with routing button and operational alerts.
