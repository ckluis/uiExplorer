Warmth Threads · Keepsake Stack

v5 Opus 4.8 Fully Autonomous
high Claude Code autonomous

Purpose

A variant of Warmth Threads for Tend — the conversation history beneath the unread/debt line, so you remember who someone is before you triage them. Part of a 5-variant autonomous research series.

Variant Focus

A tactile keepsake stack where memories are layered, slightly-rotated paper note-cards that fan open like a shoebox of mementos rather than a digital timeline.

Prompt

This version was generated autonomously by a sub-agent in a fully-autonomous origination round. The prompt below is the exact prompt given to the build agent — crafted by the orchestrating workflow from the OG master prompt and the ideation phase.

Build a standalone Tend variant called KEEPSAKE STACK — one of 5 interpretations of the Warmth Threads feature. NO JavaScript. Pure HTML/CSS only. Exactly 375×720, mobile-only, wrapped in .tnd-app.

FEATURE: Warmth Threads adds the warm history underneath Tend's debt line. This variant makes that history TACTILE and physical — memory as keepsake.

YOUR ANGLE — PHYSICAL KEEPSAKE STACK:
Each person-card, when opened (CSS checkbox + :checked), reveals its memories not as a flat timeline but as a STACK of small physical keepsake cards — like mementos in a shoebox. Collapsed: the card shows a tiny peeking stack (2-3 paper edges fanned at the bottom with slight rotate() and box-shadows, 'tap to remember'). Expanded (max-height/transform transition): the stack fans out into a readable vertical run of note-cards, each one a small parchment 'memento' with: slight per-card rotation (rotate(-1.5deg) / rotate(1deg) alternating for hand-placed feel), soft paper drop-shadow, a date in Fraunces, a glyph (☎ ✉ ◆ ♡), and the remembered-context line. Add tactile touches with pure CSS: a faint torn/taped top edge, subtle inner texture via layered gradients, a slightly different paper tint per type. PERSONAL ♡ keepsakes are the special ones: warm amber paper, a tiny pressed-heart sticker, sitting proudest in the stack — like a polaroid you kept. Transactional ones are plainer, cooler paper.

The relationship-age line at the bottom should read like a label on the box: sage Fraunces 'You've tended Maria for 14 months.' Keep the triage verb reachable in the thumb zone.

Wrap it in the normal Tend inbox: sticky pressure-context header + debt-ranked feed (heat edges, monograms, debt lines, triage verbs). Author ONE card pre-fanned showing the full keepsake stack with a glowing personal memento; others collapsed to their peeking-stack state, tappable via :checked. Lean hard into the physicality — rotations, shadows, paper — while staying within the warm Tend palette. This is the most material/sentimental of the 5 variants.

HONEST PROOF STATE: hero card checkbox checked by default to show the fanned stack; others show the collapsed peeking-stack affordance.

PALETTE (scope to .tnd-app, NOT :root): --bg:#faf7f2; --bg2:#f4eee4; --card:#fffdf9; --ink:#2b2622; --ink2:#6f675e; --ink3:#9b9288; --line:#ece4d7; --cold:#6b8cae; --warm:#e0a23b; --urgent:#e06a5b; --green:#3da06b; --accent:#2f9e8f; --accent-soft:#dff0ed. Fonts: Fraunces + Inter via Google Fonts. Deliver one self-contained HTML file.
