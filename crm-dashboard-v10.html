CRM Dashboard

v10 Fully Autonomous

Purpose

A mobile-first, card-triage CRM dashboard for real estate agents. Uses a stacked deck metaphor where leads are presented one at a time for rapid decisioning — snooze, inspect, or act immediately. Designed to feel like swiping through a pipeline.

Variant Focus

"Triage" — Card Stack/Mobile-First. Swipe through your pipeline. Core metaphor is a deck of cards presented one at a time with three large action buttons. Extreme information hierarchy with contact info, lead score badges, and recommended actions.

Prompt

This version was generated autonomously by a sub-agent. The prompt below is the exact prompt given to the build agent — it was crafted by the orchestrating agent based on the origination prompt and research phase.

v10: "Triage" — Card Stack/Mobile-First

Tagline: "Swipe through your pipeline."
Core metaphor: A deck of cards or Tinder — leads presented one at a time, agent makes rapid decisions.

Layout: Centered large card (~420px wide, ~550px tall) with rounded corners and shadow. Behind it, 2-3 cards visible in fanned stack (offset, scaled via CSS transform). Below: three large action buttons (Snooze amber, Details blue, Act Now green). Above: progress bar ("7 of 23"). Card shows contact photo placeholder, name, key context, recommended action in large text.

Key UI elements: Stacked card deck with depth (3 cards, progressive offset, scale, z-index). Action triptych buttons. Extreme information hierarchy (name 24px, action 18px). Segmented progress bar. Category tabs. Quick-stat circular badges. Swipe hint CSS animations. Deep gradient background.

Color/mood: Bold, high-contrast, mobile-optimized. Background: deep purple-to-blue gradient. Cards: clean white, generous padding, large typography. Saturated chunky action buttons. Playful but productive.

Role toggle: Solo Agent has personal task deck. Team Lead gains "My Queue / Assign Queue" toggle. Brokerage Admin gets review/approval queue with Approve, Flag, Reassign actions.
