Warmth Threads · Warmth Ledger

v4 Opus 4.8 Fully Autonomous
high Claude Code autonomous

Purpose

A variant of Warmth Threads for Tend — the conversation history beneath the unread/debt line, so you remember who someone is before you triage them. Part of a 5-variant autonomous research series.

Variant Focus

A two-rail ledger that splits the thread into a glowing personal-memory column and a muted transactional column, visualizing the warmth-versus-business balance of the relationship.

Prompt

This version was generated autonomously by a sub-agent in a fully-autonomous origination round. The prompt below is the exact prompt given to the build agent — crafted by the orchestrating workflow from the OG master prompt and the ideation phase.

Build a standalone Tend variant called WARMTH LEDGER — one of 5 interpretations of the Warmth Threads feature. NO JavaScript. Pure HTML/CSS only. Exactly 375×720, mobile-only, wrapped in .tnd-app.

FEATURE: Warmth Threads adds the warm history underneath Tend's debt line. This variant gives that history STRUCTURE: it answers 'is this relationship all business, or is there warmth in it?'

YOUR ANGLE — TWO-RAIL RELATIONSHIP LEDGER:
Thread expands from a card (CSS checkbox + :checked, downward max-height grow within the inbox feed) but instead of one timeline, it renders as a DUAL-RAIL ledger. A central time spine runs vertically; memories hang off two sides: the WARM/PERSONAL side (♡ moments) glowing in amber on --card with warm wash, and the TRANSACTIONAL side (☎ calls, ✉ notes, ◆ meetings) in cool muted ink on the other rail. Each entry still carries a Fraunces date and a one-line remembered context. The visual asymmetry of the two rails IS the insight: a person with a fat warm rail and thin transactional rail reads as a true friendship; the reverse reads as a relationship that's gone cold-transactional and needs a human touch.

Add a small humane 'balance' indicator at the top of the expanded thread — a pure-CSS split bar (two flex divs, amber vs slate, widths reflecting the ratio) labeled '3 warm · 5 business', so the warmth balance is glanceable. CRITICAL: keep it humane, not a cold dashboard — Fraunces for emotional text, warm parchment surfaces, no charts-for-charts'-sake. The personal column must clearly visually dominate/glow. End with the sage Fraunces age line 'You've tended Maria for 14 months.' Triage verb stays pinned in the thumb zone.

Show a normal Tend inbox around it: sticky pressure-context header + debt-ranked feed with heat edges, monograms, debt lines. Author ONE card pre-expanded showing the full two-rail ledger with the balance bar; others collapsed and tappable.

HONEST PROOF STATE: hero card's checkbox checked by default to reveal the ledger; remaining cards collapsed.

PALETTE (scope to .tnd-app, NOT :root): --bg:#faf7f2; --bg2:#f4eee4; --card:#fffdf9; --ink:#2b2622; --ink2:#6f675e; --ink3:#9b9288; --line:#ece4d7; --cold:#6b8cae; --warm:#e0a23b; --urgent:#e06a5b; --green:#3da06b; --accent:#2f9e8f; --accent-soft:#dff0ed. Fonts: Fraunces + Inter via Google Fonts. Deliver one self-contained HTML file.
