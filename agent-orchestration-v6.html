Human/Agent Orchestration

v6 Fully Autonomous

Purpose

Exploring agent orchestration as a restaurant kitchen brigade de cuisine — a top-down view of a professional kitchen mid-service, where the Chef at the pass orchestrates station chefs and commis to build an analytics dashboard. Part of a 10-variant research series.

Variant Focus

Kitchen Brigade — the human is the dining room sending orders, the Chef (orchestrator) stands at the pass coordinating all stations. Each station chef (Saucier, Poissonnier, Rôtisseur, Pâtissier, Garde Manger, Entremetier) is a sub-agent working a specific domain. Commis are sub-sub-agents. A ticket rail shows task decomposition. When a question arises, the Runner carries it to the pass and the Chef calls "86!" to halt the line.
